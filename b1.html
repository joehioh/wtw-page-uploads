Build The Fit

The Standard DivaDance Outfit

Three pieces. That's the whole formula. Anything stretchy on the bottom, anything comfortable on top, and a flat-soled sneaker you can pivot in.

01

Bottoms

  • Leggings
  • Joggers
  • Biker shorts
  • Athletic shorts
  • Sweatpants
  • Anything with stretch

Skip:The choreography includes turns, hip movement, and floor work, so anything that restricts your hips or knees is going to make class harder than it needs to be.

02

Tops

  • Tank
  • T-shirt
  • Sports bra
  • Cropped tee
  • Oversized tee
  • Whatever you wore to the gym last week

Heads up:Layers are fine if you tend to run cold. The room warms up fast once we start moving.

03

Shoes

  • Flat-soled sneakers
  • Converse
  • Vans
  • Adidas Sambas
  • Low-profile cross-trainers

Skip:Thick-treaded running shoes. The grip fights your turns.
